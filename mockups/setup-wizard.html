Bridge Configuration

NetTap will create a transparent Layer 2 bridge between your selected WAN and LAN interfaces. This adds no latency to your network and makes the appliance invisible on the data path.

ISP Modem WAN
enp1s0
NetTap br0
enp2s0
Router LAN
The bridge interface br0 will bind enp1s0 (WAN) and enp2s0 (LAN) in transparent forwarding mode. All traffic passes through unmodified while NetTap captures a copy for analysis.
